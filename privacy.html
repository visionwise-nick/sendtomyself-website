Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and this policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information

Information Collection

Send To Myself is committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect information that is necessary to provide quality service:

Types of Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: App feature usage, performance data
  • File Metadata: File names, sizes, types (excluding file content)
  • Network Information: IP address, connection status

🔒 Important Notice

We do NOT collect or store your file content, personal messages, or sensitive information. All file transfers occur directly between your devices.

Information Usage

The information we collect is used only for the following purposes:

Service Provision

  • Provide cross-device file synchronization functionality
  • Optimize app performance and stability
  • Provide customer support services
  • Ensure secure app operation

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Fix technical issues and bugs

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data is prioritized for local device storage
  • Minimum Permission Principle: Only request permissions necessary for app operation
  • Regular Security Updates: Continuously update security measures and protocols

🛡️ Security Assurance

We use advanced security technologies including SSL/TLS encryption, data anonymization, and access controls to ensure your information security.

Information Sharing

We are committed to not selling, trading, or otherwise transferring your personal information to third parties, except when:

  • We have your explicit consent
  • Required by law or court order
  • Protecting our rights, property, or security
  • Working with trusted service providers (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access Right: View information we hold about you
  • Correction Right: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion Right: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction Right: Restrict our processing of your information
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preference settings
  • Analyze website usage
  • Provide personalized experience

You can control cookie usage through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or email. Continued use of our service indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
