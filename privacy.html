Introduction

Send To Myself ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our cross-device file sharing and message memory assistant application.

Information We Collect

Device Information

  • Device ID and type
  • Operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics

User Content

  • Files, images, videos, and documents you choose to share
  • Text messages and chat history
  • Group information and member lists

Technical Data

  • Network connection information
  • App performance and crash data
  • Usage analytics to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Enable cross-device file transfer
  • Synchronize messages between your devices
  • Manage group functionality
  • Provide memory management features

Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Develop new features

Security

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure service reliability
  • Protect user data

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Files and messages are stored locally on your devices. We do not permanently store your content on our servers.

Temporary Server Storage

  • Files are temporarily stored during transfer between devices
  • Messages are cached for synchronization purposes
  • All data is automatically deleted after successful transfer

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for file transfers
  • Secure WebSocket connections
  • Regular security audits

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@sendtomyself.app
  • Website: https://sendtomyself.app

Data Collection Summary

Data We Collect

  • Device information for service functionality
  • User-generated content (files, messages) for cross-device transfer
  • Usage analytics for app improvement

How We Use Data

  • Provide core file sharing and messaging services
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure service security and reliability

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your devices
  • Server data: Automatically deleted after transfer
  • Analytics: Anonymized and retained for service improvement

Third-Party Sharing

  • No data sold to third parties
  • No advertising partnerships
  • No analytics sharing with external parties
